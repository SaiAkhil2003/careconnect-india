# Provider Listing Consent Text

Use this wording in email, WhatsApp, forms, or call notes when collecting provider consent.

## Short Consent Wording

The provider confirms that CareConnect India may display the provider name, city, areas served, service categories, phone number, email address, website if provided, and listing details on the CareConnect India platform.

CareConnect India may contact the provider for corrections, updates, verification, and listing-related communication. The provider can request updates or removal of the listing by contacting CareConnect India.

Premium or paid listing features are separate and optional. Consent to a basic listing does not require the provider to buy a paid plan.

## Suggested Confirmation Message

Please reply with:

```text
I confirm that I am authorised to approve this listing, and I consent to CareConnect India displaying our provider details on the CareConnect India platform.
```

Record the name, role, contact method, and date of the consent confirmation.
