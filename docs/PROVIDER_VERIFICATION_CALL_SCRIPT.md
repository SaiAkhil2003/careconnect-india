# Provider Verification Call Script

Use this script to verify real provider details before import or publication. Do not mark a provider as approved during the call; approval happens after internal review.

## Opening

Hello, my name is [name]. I am calling from CareConnect India. We are building a city-wise aged care provider discovery platform for families. I would like to verify your organisation details and ask whether you consent to being listed. This should take a few minutes.

## Verification Questions

1. Can you confirm the official provider or organisation name we should list?
2. Which city are you based in, and which areas or neighbourhoods do you serve?
3. Which services do you offer for older adults?
   - Home care.
   - Senior living or assisted living.
   - Day care.
   - Physiotherapy or rehab.
   - Geriatric doctor or clinic services.
   - Companion care.
   - Dementia care.
4. Do you provide home visits, facility-based care, or both?
5. Which languages can your team support for families or patients?
6. What phone number and email should families use to contact you?
7. Is there a specific lead receiving email or WhatsApp number CareConnect India should use for enquiries?
8. Do you agree that CareConnect India may list your provider name, city, areas served, services, contact details, website if provided, and listing details on the CareConnect India platform?
9. Would you like information later about Free, Standard, or Premium listing options?
10. Are you authorised to confirm these listing details on behalf of the provider?

## Closing

Thank you. Provider data will be reviewed before public listing. If anything needs correction later, you can ask CareConnect India to update or remove your listing.

## Internal Notes

After the call, record:

- Caller name.
- Contact person name and role.
- Date and time.
- Verification status.
- Consent status.
- Services confirmed.
- Areas confirmed.
- Any uncertainty or follow-up required.
